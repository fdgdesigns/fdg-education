Entertainment Industry Education

About the Project

With over nine years of experience in the theatre industry, I've noticed that many design and production students struggle to learn more about the industry due to a lack of resources and support.To address this, I decided to create free educational courses drawing from my own journey of self-learning and mentorship. With the goal to help them advance their careers, focusing on computer software used in the entertainment industry, particularly lighting design. These courses aim to bridge the skills gap and inspire creativity by covering essential tools for film editing, audio production, lighting design/programming, and graphic design. Additionally, a resource website will be developed to provide accessible educational materials, tutorials, expert insights, and community forums to support and inspire theatre production and design enthusiasts.

NOTE: This site is designed to be continuously updated with new courses, lessons, and content as I gain more knowledge and experience in the entertainment industry.
